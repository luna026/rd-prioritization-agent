Promising directions — June 18, 2026

Gradient and adaptive field conditions (EXP007, EXP010, EXP012) have shown the best
balance between alignment and stability so far. EXP012 achieved both a good alignment
score (0.67) and good stability (0.64) but was expensive (220 EUR) and slow (9 hours).
Next experiments should explore whether adaptive field at slightly reduced density can
keep the same trade-off at lower cost and time.
